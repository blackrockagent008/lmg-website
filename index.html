A Texas Premier Go-To Contractor

LMG General Contractors is a regional general contracting and management firm headquartered in Houston, TX. Since 2006, we have delivered exceptional results across hospitality, retail, multifamily, commercial, and specialty construction sectors throughout Texas. Our services cover every project phase — from feasibility and permitting through delivery and closeout.

Leveraging People, Processes, Partnerships, and Technology, LMG enables real-time collaboration so all decisions are made on the most accurate, up-to-date information available.
